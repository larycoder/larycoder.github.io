Philosophy

I has only 1 rule for almost everything in my daily life: Simple as possible. But that is not all my philosophy. During living and studying time. I concluded 3 things:

  • Clarify what you will do
  • Done everything step by step
  • Log out job process
Let me explain: 3 conclusion I recognized base on 1 philosophy - simple as possible. First, clarify what you will do defines the target of job. And all your task should be homogeneous with that target. So it let your job simple with only 1 target at a time. Advise is that you should work for 1 target only and don't lose it in middle. Second, Done everything step by step help you outline strategy of the job. It is easy to track and follow. Sometimes, your strategy is not best, non optimized. But the most importance that it simple for you to following your plan. After that, you can improve it through time depending on the skelenton that you has ready done. Final, Log out job process is newest law I found out. You can use it or not, but it help me a lot on reload the process into my short memory in each time working. To make it clearly, imagine when you do 1 job for multiple of days. Each day, you did part of job. Then next time, you need to remember the part of work has not yet done. And you can accidentally forgot somethign. To solve this problem and make process simple, I will log out my job, my idea, what I did and what I intended to do. In next day, I will use that log to remind me the state that I stopped before. It speed up my job a lot.
